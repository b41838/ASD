About iPill Keeper

What is iPill Keeper?

iPill Keeper is an app designed (but not limited) to keep track of the pills a diabetic takes. iPill Keeper will track any pills and other basic data that you provide for safe keeping.

Check us out on GitHub!

iPill Keeper is an open project hosted at GitHub.
